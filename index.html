About the Park

Hampden Industrial Park has been a home for Sheridan-area trades and businesses since the first building went up in 1973. Three additional buildings were added in 1986, bringing the park to 120,216 gross square feet across 8.2 acres.

The park is made up of 83 tenant spaces — a mix of warehouse, flex, office-warehouse combos, and single-bay garages. Construction is precast concrete wall panels over a steel frame, with slab-on-grade floors and one overhead door per tenant. Several units are set up with exhaust ports for mechanic and automotive use.

The whole property is professionally managed onsite by Catalyst Real Estate, and zoned for commercial use within the City of Sheridan.

Location & Access

Right on West Hampden Avenue with direct shots to US-285, Santa Fe Drive, and the wider Denver metro.

Hampden Industrial Park sits on West Hampden Avenue (US-285) in Sheridan, just a few minutes from Santa Fe Drive (US-85) and an easy drive to I-25, C-470, and downtown Denver. The site is built for fleet, delivery, and service businesses that need to move across the metro without fighting highway congestion.

Nearby supply houses, equipment dealers, restaurants, and fuel stops make this a convenient base for contractors and service crews working Denver and the south metro.
